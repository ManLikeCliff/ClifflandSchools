Our Curriculum

The curriculum (British and Nigerian Curricula) for Cliffland Secondary Schools, is modern and challenges and inspires learners to be adaptable and flexible. The Curriculum is coherent with the needs of learners, Nigeria and the broader international society.

Elementary

Children admitted are usually between the ages of two and five for the Pre-school and Reception classes, while children between ages five and six are for the primary grades. It aims at educating young learners between the ages of 2 and 11years, impacting them through creative and modern methods of teaching to deliver an all-round education.

Intermediate

Our curriculum includes some special programmes embedded which are aimed at making the learners versatile. A range of skills from programmes such as Etiquette and Grooming, Digital Math, Co-Curricular Activities, Lifelong learning skills, Leadership training and Safety and security tips contribute in identifying hidden talents and also give learners competitive advantages in the larger world.

Pre-degree College

Our culture of maintaining a high standard of academic excellence has commanded the respect of our peers in the education sector. Our approach to teaching and learning is geared towards problem-solving; hence, we encourage project-based learning. And our students’ stellar performances in both local and international examinations have shown the effectiveness of our approach.
